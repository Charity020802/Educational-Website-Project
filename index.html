Science & Geography Centre

Welcome to the Science and Geography Centre, where we are dedicated to providing high-quality education and support to students in the fields of science and geography. Our mission is to empower learners with the knowledge and skills they need to excel academically and pursue successful careers in these exciting disciplines. Whether you're a student looking for tutoring, a parent seeking resources, or an educator interested in collaboration, we are here to help you achieve your goals. We offer tutoring in Natural, Life, and Physical Sciences, as well as Geography, for students in grade 8-12. We teach and simplify the content, provide you with study material, prepare you for examinations via live sessions, YouTube videos and one-on-one sessions.

Natural Science Classes

Grade 8-9

Natural Sciences for Grades 8–9 provides foundational knowledge in biology, chemistry, physics, and earth sciences. The course includes topics such as cells and body systems, ecosystems, matter and materials, chemical reactions, energy transfer, forces and motion, and the structure of the Earth. Emphasis is placed on scientific investigations, experimental skills, data interpretation, and critical thinking.

Physical Science Classes

Grade 10-12

Our Physical Science program aims to equip learners from Grade 10–12 with a solid understanding of the fundamental principles of physics and chemistry. Through interactive lessons, practical experiments, and real-world applications, students explore concepts such as energy, matter, forces, and chemical reactions. The program develops critical thinking, analytical skills, and scientific curiosity, preparing learners to confidently engage with science and technology.

Life Sciences Classes

Grade 10-12

Our Life Science program aims to equip learners from Grade 10–12 with a strong foundation in biological sciences, helping them understand the natural world and the human body. Through engaging lessons, practical experiments, and critical thinking activities, students develop scientific inquiry skills, problem-solving abilities, and an appreciation for health.

Geography classes

Grade 8 & 9

Our Geography program aims to equip learners from Grade 8–12 with a strong understanding of the Earth, its environments, and the relationship between people and their surroundings. Through engaging lessons, map work, case studies, and real-world investigations, students explore topics such as climate, natural resources, population, and sustainability.

Life Science Study Guide

e-Book

This study guide provides clear and structured summaries of key biological concepts, including cells, genetics, human body systems, and ecology.It simplifies complex processes using diagrams, definitions, and examples to improve understanding. The guide supports effective revision and helps learners prepare confidently for tests and examinations.
